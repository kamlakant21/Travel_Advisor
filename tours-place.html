Places to visit

Day 1

Santorini

Santorini is an island in the Aegean Sea, which is a part of Greece. It lies 200 Km southeast of Athens and 140 Km north of Crete. Covering an area of 96 square km, Santorini is a part of the Cyclades group of islands, where all the islands have similar characteristics.

Day 2

Mykonos

Popular for its party culture, Mykonos is amongst the most loved tourist places in Greece for its lovely beaches, emblematic windmills, historical structures, whitewashed facades, and stunning views of Caldera. Panagia Paraportiani Church, Archaeological Museum of Mykonos, Folklore Museum, or Lena's House Museum let you learn about the rich heritage.

Day 3

Athens

Tourist places in Athens garner great appeal and traction by the travellers at large. An ideogram of Western Civilization at its awe-inspiring best, Athens swanks illustriously the rich and glorious history that sweeps back to more than three thousand years.

Day 4

Nafplio

Complete with stunning Venetian architectural marvels, Ottoman structures to a bustling promenade, the city of Nafplio is amongst the most beautiful places to visit in Greece. The ancient walls, medieval monuments, castles, statues, and neoclassical buildings are the exemplars of unique architecture and enhance the appeal and add a distinct aura to the city.

Day 5

Delphi

Known as the 'Centre of Earth', the sanctuary of Delphi is nestled amidst lush mountains and offers a most spectacular panorama.It used to be a center of cultural and religious activities from ancient ages, and a symbol of unity as well.
